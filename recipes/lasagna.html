Lasagna

One of the most comforting foods ready to be made by you!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • 1 onion and two cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatos, 2 cans of tomato sauce and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Lasagna noodles (either bought or handmade)
  • Cheese
  • spices and seasonings (salt, pepper, dried basil leaves, oregano)

In the search for the worlds most comforting foods we are entering the italian cuisine

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

How to make Lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce by cooking the meat and then putting in the tomato products.
  2. (Optional) Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna (Noodles, meat + sauce, repeat)
  4. Sprinkle cheese over it and let it cook for about 20 min at 200deg celsius.
  5. Bon appetit!
