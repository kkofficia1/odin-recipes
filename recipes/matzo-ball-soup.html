Matzo Sall Soup

A vegetarian soup to comfort your soul

Ingredients

  • Matzo Balls
  • Chili Oil
  • Vegetable Broth

How to make Matzo Ball Soup

  1. Make vegetable broth. (you can use powder by maggi)
  2. Strain the broth.
  3. Put Matzo balls into boiling water.
  4. Take them out and serve the broth with matzo balls and carrots.
  5. Bon Appetit!
