Fresh Bread with Scrambled Eggs

Simple but still effective - Fresh bread with scrambled eggs and tomatos

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 6 Eggs
  • 2 Cherry Tomatos

How to make Bread with scrambled eggs and tomatos

  1. Toast your slice of bread.
  2. Scramble your eggs and season them.
  3. Cook them in a pan.
  4. Cut your cherry tomatos in half.
  5. Put your eggs onto your toasted bread and put your tomatos on your eggs.
  6. Bon Appetit!
